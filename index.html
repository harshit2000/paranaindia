Innovative Solutions

Interior design is an art form that is always moving and evolving into new innovations. As a result, it can be overwhelming to keep track of new trends and revolutions that are shaping its future. From new technology, to psychological suggestions, there are more and more differing perspectives and researched-back ideas that are progressing interior design.

Project Management

Our architecture and interior design project management process often starts with an intensive, multi-day planning period in which we meet the client to exchange ideas, explore possibilities, and develop a plan. During this phase, the client’s needs and objectives are identified. Questions regarding the specific function(s) of the space, who will be using the space and furniture and equipment requirements are discussed. Measurements and photos are also taken at this time if required.

About Us

A Creative Architecture Agency For Your Dream Space

Paraná Interiors, was founded in the year 2016, by the visionary entrepreneur and industry mentor Ms. Poonam Gupta & Mr. Sumit Singh Baghel. Under their vision the company in a short span of over 6 years has carved its niche in the segment of high end interiors for hospitality, commercial, and private sector projects with its unwavering commitment towards integrity and transparency, bringing to life inspiring spaces that fuse form and function seamlessly. The company is defined by its focus on perfection and innovations, which is complemented by the clarity of vision that only a skilled team of professionals can bring. DESIGN APPROACH What sets us apart is our client centric approach which enables us to work with integrity and constantly aiming for excellence. This strategy has enabled us to achieve an extremely high customer retention rate, showcased by 70% of our annual business coming from existing clients. We engage our clients with the best consultants who ensure adherence to budget and timelines.

INNOVATION SOLUTIONS Paraná has expanded its clientele from corporate offices to hospitals and hotels and residentials projects. We have also diversified our service offering beyond interior solutions, to offer comprehensive fit-out solutions as well as General Contracting with all its related services and IT packages. Capable of delivering offices of 1 million square feet and above on a General Contracting basis. Created over 25 million square feet of workspace seating more than half a million professionals across the country. PROJECT MANAGEMENT Paraná, having a strong presence over 10 cities, our team consists of highly skilled professionals such as Architects, Engineers, Procurement team and certified project managers to give business a technical edge.
